Easy Homemade Pizza Dough


Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners,
you're in luck.
This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim and it puts
the store-bought stuff to shame.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Place warm water in a bowl
  4. add yeast and sugar.
  5. Mix and let stand until creamy
  6. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture
