Smoked Salmon Sushi Roll


Description

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice,
nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi.
It might take a while to learn how to make a perfect roll,
but you'll have fun trying.

Ingredients

  • Japanese sushi rice
  • rice wine vinegar
  • nori
  • wasabi paste
  • smoked salmon

Steps

  1. Soak rice
  2. mix in rice wine vinegar
  3. Place 1 sheet of seaweed on a bamboo mat
  4. press a thin layer of cool rice on seaweed
  5. arrange
  6. roll up tightly
